🚀 Adventure Works Customer Analytics Dashboard
📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard built using the Adventure Works dataset, focused on customer analytics and business performance insights.
The dashboard is designed to help stakeholders understand sales trends, customer behavior, and product performance through visually rich and dynamic reports.

🎯 Objectives
Analyze customer purchasing behavior
Track sales performance and KPIs
Identify top customers and products
Monitor regional sales distribution
Compare actual vs target performance
🛠️ Tools & Technologies
Power BI – Dashboard development & visualization
Excel / SQL – Data preprocessing and transformation
📊 Dashboard Overview

The report consists of 4 interactive pages:

📍 1. Executive Summary

Provides a high-level overview of business performance with key KPIs and trends:

KPIs: Total Revenue, Total Profit, Total Orders, Return Rate
Revenue Over Time (Line Chart)
Total Orders by Customer (Clustered Bar Chart)
Monthly Revenue & Monthly Orders
Top 10 Products by Revenue
🌍 2. Map Analysis

Geographical representation of sales performance:

Sales distribution across regions:
Europe
North America
Pacific
Interactive map visualization by country
👥 3. Customer Summary

Insights into customer segmentation and behavior:

Total Orders by Income Category (Pie Chart)
Revenue by Education Category
Top Revenue-Generating Customers
Year-wise Revenue Trends
Detailed Customer Information
📦 4. Product Summary

Analysis of product performance and targets:

Total Orders vs Target Orders (Gauge Chart)
Total Revenue vs Target Revenue (Gauge Chart)
Total Profit vs Target Profit (Gauge Chart)
Monthly Profit Trend (Area Chart)
Top 10 Products by Revenue
📈 Key Insights
Identified high-value customers and segments
Highlighted top-performing products
Analyzed regional sales trends
Compared actual performance vs targets
Tracked monthly growth patterns
