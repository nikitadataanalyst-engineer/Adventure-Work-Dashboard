# 🚀 Adventure Works Customer Analytics Dashboard<br>

## 📌 Project Overview<br>  
This project presents an **interactive Power BI dashboard** built using the **Adventure Works dataset**, focused on **customer analytics and business performance insights**.<br>  
The dashboard helps stakeholders understand sales trends, customer behavior, and product performance through dynamic and visually rich reports.<br><br>

---

## 🎯 Objectives<br>  
- Analyze **customer purchasing behavior**<br>  
- Track **sales performance and KPIs**<br>  
- Identify **top customers and products**<br>  
- Monitor **regional sales distribution**<br>  
- Compare **actual vs target performance**<br><br>

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies<br>  
- **Power BI** – Dashboard development & visualization<br>  
- **Excel ** – Data cleaning and transformation<br><br>

---

## 📊 Dashboard Overview<br>  
The report consists of **4 interactive Tabs**:<br><br>

---

### 📍 1. Executive Summary<br>  
Provides a high-level overview of business performance:<br><br>

- **KPIs**: Total Revenue, Total Profit, Total Orders, Return Rate<br>  
- **Revenue Over Time** (Line Chart)<br>  
- **Total Orders by Customer** (Clustered Bar Chart)<br>  
- **Monthly Revenue & Monthly Orders**<br>  
- **Top 10 Products by Revenue**<br><br>

---

### 🌍 2. Map Analysis<br>  
Geographical representation of sales performance:<br><br>

- Sales distribution across:<br>  
  - Europe<br>  
  - North America<br>  
  - Pacific<br>  
- Interactive map visualization by country<br><br>

---

### 👥 3. Customer Summary<br>  
Customer segmentation and insights:<br><br>

- **Total Orders by Income Category** (Pie Chart)<br>  
- **Revenue by Education Category**<br>  
- **Top Revenue Customers**<br>  
- **Year-wise Revenue Trends**<br>  
- Customer details and information<br><br>

---

### 📦 4. Product Summary<br>  
Product performance and target comparison:<br><br>

- **Total Orders vs Target Orders** (Gauge Chart)<br>  
- **Total Revenue vs Target Revenue** (Gauge Chart)<br>  
- **Total Profit vs Target Profit** (Gauge Chart)<br>  
- **Monthly Profit Trend** (Area Chart)<br>  
- **Top 10 Products by Revenue**<br><br>

---

## 📈 Key Insights<br>  
- Identified **high-value customers and segments**<br>  
- Highlighted **top-performing products**<br>  
- Analyzed **regional sales trends**<br>  
- Compared **actual performance vs targets**<br>  
- Tracked **monthly growth patterns**<br><br>
